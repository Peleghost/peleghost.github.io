About Me


Hello, I'm Fellipe, a dedicated software developer with a passion for creating innovative and impactful solutions. With a strong commitment to staying at the forefront of technology, I continuously research and adopt emerging tools and methodologies while appreciating the enduring value of foundational technologies that shape the industry today.


My core expertise lies in C# (C Sharp), a versatile and powerful programming language that enables the development of robust applications across various platforms, including desktop, web, mobile, and cross-platform environments. In addition to my proficiency in C#, I have experience working with TypeScript, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and Linux systems, which further enhance my ability to deliver comprehensive and effective solutions.


What drives me is the opportunity to leverage technology to solve complex problems, streamline processes, and create meaningful user experiences. My approach is rooted in a dedication to delivering high-quality results that exceed expectations and contribute positively to society.


I began my journey as a self-taught developer before pursuing formal education in software development. Along the way, I gained valuable experience through freelance projects, which honed my technical skills and adaptability. My background also includes leadership experience in managerial roles outside the tech industry, where I enhanced my communication skills and the ability to collaborate effectively with diverse teams.


Looking ahead, my career goal is to contribute to meaningful software solutions that make a positive impact on society and the world. Whether it's through innovative applications or transformative systems, I am eager to be part of projects that drive progress and create lasting value.
